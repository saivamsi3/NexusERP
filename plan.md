Your current NexusERP is feature-rich, but it still reads like a generic ERP roadmap. The hackathon judges will already see Product, Sales, Purchase, Inventory, Manufacturing, and BoM in almost every ERP submission.

The winning approach is to redesign the project around **Shiv Furniture's actual pain points**.

# NexusERP

## Intelligent Demand-to-Delivery Manufacturing Operating System

### Core Problem

Shiv Furniture is not suffering because they don't have software.

They are suffering because they don't have visibility.

The real problem is:

* Sales does not know stock.
* Purchase does not know shortages.
* Manufacturing does not know priorities.
* Inventory is inaccurate.
* Management cannot see business status.

NexusERP solves this by creating a single source of truth for the entire factory.

---
